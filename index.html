Privacy Policy for LB Food Scanner

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

Thank you for using LB Food Scanner ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information, what third-party services it may use, and what choices you have. This page is intended to apply globally to the Android and iPhone versions of the App, depending on platform, app version, and enabled settings. It also includes basic terms of use.

1. The Core Principle

The App is built to be privacy-friendly.

  • No account is required to use the App.
  • No third-party ads are shown in the App.
  • The majority of your data, including scan history, saved product notes, allergen preferences, digestive-profile preferences, scan mode, enabled databases, and most settings, is stored only on your device.
  • Some features depend on third-party services such as product databases, translation services, cosmetic/chemical reference sources, donation pages, and your device’s built-in speech services. Those services may receive limited data needed to provide the feature you choose to use.

2. Information We Process and How We Use It

To provide its features, the App needs to access or process certain information.

a. Information processed on your device

This information is primarily handled locally on your device.

  • Camera Access:
    • Why we need it: The App supports barcode scanning and manual product scanning from photos, including food ingredient lists, pet food ingredient lists, cosmetic INCI lists, and cleaning product labels.
    • How it is used: The camera is only used when you actively scan a product or take a photo for text recognition.
    • On-device processing: Depending on your platform and app version, barcode scanning, text recognition, language identification, image crop assistance, and related features may use Google ML Kit on Android and/or Apple system frameworks and translation-related system services on iPhone.
    • Temporary files: Temporary image files, crop data, or cache files may be created during scan processing or image handling on your device.
  • Scan History:
    • What it is: The App can save recently scanned products so you can view them again later.
    • How it is stored: Stored only on your device’s app storage.
    • Your control: You can disable history saving or clear saved history in the App settings.
  • Per-Product Notes:
    • What it is: You can save your own note for a scanned product, for example to remember packaging details, missing allergen information, digestive triggers, sensitivities, or personal reminders.
    • How it is stored: Notes are saved locally on your device only, usually per barcode or per scanned product key.
    • Who can see it: Only the user of that device/app install. Notes are not shared with other users.
  • Translation Cache:
    • What it is: The App may store cached translations locally to avoid repeating translation requests and to improve speed.
    • How it is stored: Stored only on your device.
  • Product Images and Cached Media:
    • If a product database provides an image URL, the App may load that image and cache it locally on your device for display performance.
  • Preferences and Settings:
    • Examples include selected allergens, digestive-profile settings (such as IBS / SIBO, fructose, polyol, onion / garlic, inulin / FOS, custom digestive triggers, and Biofarm / Cleaner Label preferences), nutritional alert toggles, preferred translation engine, voice mode, speech rate and pitch, dark mode, selected scan mode, and enabled product databases.
    • These are stored locally on your device.
  • Voice Assistant / Text-to-Speech:
    • If you enable Voice Assistant Mode, the App sends text to your device’s configured speech engine so results can be read aloud.
    • Depending on your platform and version, this may use Android Text-to-Speech, Apple speech / voice services, or another installed device speech engine.
    • Your device’s speech engine may operate under its own privacy policy and terms.

b. Information shared with third-party services

Some features require limited data to be sent to third-party services. We do not require an account from you to use these features.

  • Human food barcode lookups:
    • When you scan a human food barcode, the barcode may be sent to Open Food Facts and Pro Open Food Facts to retrieve product information.
    • If you manually enable the optional legacy source in Settings, the barcode may also be sent to FoodRepo.org.
  • Pet food barcode lookups:
    • When you scan a pet food barcode, the barcode may be sent to Open Pet Food Facts to retrieve product information.
  • Cosmetic barcode lookups:
    • When you scan a cosmetic barcode, the barcode may be sent to Open Beauty Facts to retrieve product information.
  • Cleaning product barcode lookups:
    • When you scan a cleaning product barcode, the barcode may be sent to Open Products Facts to retrieve product information.
  • Translation services:
    • Depending on your platform, app version, and chosen settings, translation may use Google ML Kit, Apple Translate or related Apple system translation services, LibreTranslate, or Lingva Translate.
    • If you choose an online translator, the text you choose to translate may be sent to that selected third-party service.
    • Some translation models or language packages may be downloaded to your device when needed.
  • Cosmetic ingredient research features:
    • If you tap a cosmetic ingredient card to load extra reference data, the ingredient name may be sent to PubChem to retrieve chemical reference information.
    • If you choose to open CosIng, ECHA, or EWG Skin Deep, the App may open those services in your browser. Your use of those sites is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.
  • Source product pages and contribution websites:
    • If you choose to open a product’s source page or use a "Contribute on Website" option, the App may open an external website in your browser and include the barcode or product code in the destination URL.
  • Optional donations / support:
    • If you tap the Donate option, the App may open PayPal or Wise in your browser or app so you can support the creator voluntarily.
    • Donations are optional and do not unlock premium features, faster scans, badges, or digital extras inside the App.
  • Email contact and support:
    • If you choose to send us an email, your email app/provider will process your email address and message contents in the normal way.
    • We will receive whatever information you voluntarily include in that email and use it only to respond, investigate the issue, or provide support.

3. No Account, No Ads, Optional Support

  • The App does not require you to create an account.
  • The App does not show third-party ads.
  • The App is designed to work without a cloud profile or centralized user account.
  • Optional donations may be offered through external services, but they do not change the App’s core functionality or grant digital benefits.

4. Summary of Third-Party Services

The App may use or link to the following third-party tools, services, libraries, and public databases, depending on your platform, app version, and settings:

  • Google ML Kit: Barcode scanning, OCR/text recognition, language identification, and translation support on supported Android versions.
  • Apple system frameworks / Apple Translate / Apple speech services: Supported iPhone scanning, translation, and speech-related features where available.
  • Android Text-to-Speech: Reads results aloud on supported Android devices when voice features are enabled.
  • Open Food Facts / Pro Open Food Facts: Human food barcode data.
  • FoodRepo.org: Optional legacy human food barcode source if enabled by the user.
  • Open Pet Food Facts: Pet food barcode data.
  • Open Beauty Facts: Cosmetic barcode data.
  • Open Products Facts: Cleaning product and non-food product barcode data.
  • LibreTranslate / Lingva Translate: Optional translation providers selected by the user.
  • PubChem: Cosmetic ingredient chemical reference lookups.
  • CosIng, ECHA, and EWG Skin Deep: External research/reference resources that may be opened from the App.
  • PayPal / Wise: Optional external donation pages if the user chooses to support the App’s creator.
  • Coil: Image loading and caching library used inside the App.

5. Important Accuracy, Allergy, Digestive, and Safety Notice

  • The App relies in part on third-party and community-maintained product databases. Product information can be incomplete, outdated, or inaccurate.
  • A valid real-world product may not yet appear in a free public database, even if its barcode is correct.
  • Allergen, ingredient, digestive-trigger, cosmetic, pet food, or cleaning-product information may be missing or incomplete in online database entries.
  • The App may generate warnings, ingredient insights, translations, voice summaries, digestive-profile alerts, cleaner-label alerts, or note-based reminders, but these are informational only.
  • Always check the real product packaging and manufacturer information, especially for allergies, intolerances, IBS / SIBO / digestive sensitivities, medical conditions, pet safety, chemical exposure concerns, or skin sensitivities.
  • The App does not provide medical, nutritional, veterinary, dermatological, toxicological, regulatory, or legal advice.
  • Cosmetic and chemical reference tools such as PubChem, CosIng, ECHA, and EWG are provided for research convenience only and may have their own limitations, notices, and rules.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Camera Permission: You can deny or revoke camera access at any time in system settings.
  • History Controls: You can disable history saving and clear saved scan history inside the App.
  • Notes and Preferences: You can edit or clear your local product notes, allergen settings, digestive profiles, and other settings inside the App.
  • Translation Choice: You can choose which translation service the App uses when multiple options are offered.
  • Voice Controls: You can disable voice mode or adjust speech settings at any time.
  • Database Controls: You can enable or disable certain barcode databases in the App settings.
  • Local Data Removal: You can remove local App data by clearing App storage or uninstalling the App.
